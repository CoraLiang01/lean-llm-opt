A factory has twelve machining machines - M1, M2,... and M12 - that are required to complete twelve separate tasks A, B,... and L. Each task can only be completed on one machine at a time, and the machining costs vary depending on the machine and the task, as specified in cost_12x12.csv. The decision maker needs to select a machine for each task that minimises the total machining cost for all tasks.
